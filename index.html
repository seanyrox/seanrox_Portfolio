About

Sean

Sean Rox is an Animator/Graphic Designer & Full Stack Developer. He loves designing graphics, building websites and apps. He is born in Sweden, but now resides in Dublin for the past decade. As a Swede he gets told alot of jokes about being a viking. His goal is not to conquer the land but to conquer the digital creative space. Sean's speciality is turning nothing into something. He’s got a knack for taking an idea, giving it a foundation, then building on it to create a relevant experience for the public.
